Farm Stands & Agritourism

Farm stands and agritourism are a growing part of many successful farm businesses. OFB publishes Oregon's Bounty Farm Stand Guide each year. The guide provides easy access to OFB member farm stands and beyond. In addition, MCFB promotes local farm stands every year on this website and social media to assist local farmers in getting the word out to our communities about their offerings.

If you would like your local farm stand or agritourism opportunity listed below and publicized on the MCFB Facebook site, please Contact Us.

Bushue’s Family Farm

  • Offers: From May through June, greenhouses are filled with all the premium annuals, bedding plants, flowering baskets, patio containers and vegetable starts you need to get your garden going. U-Pick starting with strawberries in June, moving into raspberries, green beans, tomatoes and sweet and hot peppers through the summer and into the fall. Fall season brings pumpkins, hayrides, hayslide adventure park, Lil’ Pumpkin’s Playground, pig train rides, pumpkin bowling alley, dirt babies, corn trail, and farm animals.
  • Open: Flower Season (May to July): Wednesday to Saturday, 10am to 4pm. Sunday, 11am to 4pm. U-Pick Season (June to October): Depending on crop availability. Pumpkin Season (October): Tuesday to Sunday, 10am to 5pm
  • Website: bushuefarming.com
  • Address: 9880 SE Revenue Rd, Boring
  • Phone Number: 503-307-6074
