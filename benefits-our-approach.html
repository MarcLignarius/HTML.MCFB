Our Approach

MCFB's approach to fulfilling its mission and vision is accomplished through five key strategies:

  1. Asking Farmers for their input on issues facing their communities, neighbors, and other individuals.
  2. Assisting Farmers with issues they are facing, either on-farm or off of the farm.
  3. Connecting Farmers with each other through events and communications so they benefits from being part of a network of local farmers.
  4. Representing Farmers with local, state, and national officials and representatives so their voices are heard and issues addressed in laws, regulations, policies, practices, and plans.
  5. Sharing Knowledge with Farmers so they have access to training and certifications they need to keep farming alive and healthy.
