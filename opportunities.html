Farm Service Opportunities

USDA Seeks Members for National Advisory Committee on Urban Agriculture: The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) is seeking members for a new advisory committee on urban agriculture, part of a broader effort to focus on the needs of urban farmers. The 12-person committee will advise the Secretary of Agriculture on the development of policies and outreach relating to urban, indoor, and other emerging agricultural production practices as well as identify any barriers to urban agriculture. For more information, see the USDA Press Release.

MCFB Board Director Positions: MCFB's Board may be composed of four Board Officers and eight additional Board Directors. Board Directors must be MCFB Voting Members, are elected at the Annual MCFB Meeting, and serve two-year terms. The Board of Directors meets approximately eight times each year (see Board Meetings). If you’re an MCFB Voting Member and are interested in board service, please Contact Us.

MCFB Volunteer Positions: MCFB is currently recruiting for a social media volunteer. Hours and time commitment are flexible. Daily posting on Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn preferred. Additional social media development and implementation desired if possible, but not necessary. If you have an interest in this opportunity or an idea for another volunteer activity, please Contact Us.
