Find A Farm

Farm stands and agritourism are a growing part of many successful farm businesses. OFB publishes Oregon's Bounty Farm Stand Guide each year. The guide provides easy access to OFB member farm stands and beyond. In addition, MCFB promotes local farm stands every year on this website and social media to assist local farmers in getting the word out to our communities about their offerings.

If you would like your local farm stand or agritourism opportunity listed below and publicized on the MCFB Facebook site, please Contact Us.
