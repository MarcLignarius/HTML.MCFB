Contractors

MCFB contracts for services related to the executive director/secretary and treasurer.

Jay Udelhoven - Executive Director

Jay grew up in the Midwest, running around his grandparent's and uncle's dairy farms. Throughout his 30-year natural resource career, he has worked on numerous farm-related projects including dairy production in Africa, fish production in Panama, Mexico and Washington State, cattle ranching in Florida, and small-scale nursery/berry/mixed vegetable production in Oregon. Jay has served in senior level positions for several organizations over the past 20 years and has served as the MCFB Executive Director since July 2019. He holds a Bachelor of Science in Natural Resources from the University of Wisconsin-Madison and a Master of Environmental Policy from the University of Denver, Colorado.
