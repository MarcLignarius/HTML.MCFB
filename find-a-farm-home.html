Find A Farm

Support your local farmer!

If you’re looking for a farmers market, see the Oregon Farmer’s Market Association for regular season markets, online markets, COVID-safe markets, and winter/holiday markets.

If you’re looking for a local Multnomah County and Northcentral Clackamas County farm, see below!

Below you can Find A Farm near you that has U-Pick or U-Cut, on-farm retail sales, farm stands, online sales, Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) sales, or sales at your local farmer’s market.

The Find A Farm page is our attempt to identify all of the farms within Multnomah County and Northcentral Clackamas County. The farms listed below are not necessarily MCFB members nor does MCFB endorse the products and services provided by the farms. If you would like your farm listed below or information about your farm improved, please Contact Us.

For each farm, we provide information that is currently available about “Access” and “Products".

  • Access can include: U-Pick/Cut | Farm Stand/Retail | Online Sales | CSA | Farmers Market | Events | Activities
  • Products can include: Animals | Milk/Eggs | Veggies | Fruit/Berries/Pumpkins | Flowers/Herbs |Nursery | X-mas Trees | Honey | Grain/Grass | Organic/No Pesticides

Please contact the farm directly before visiting as offerings and hours often change. Also, not all farms provide onsite retail sales or public access. So be sure to call before you go!

In addition to our Find A Farm page, OFB publishes Oregon's Bounty Farm Stand Guide each year. The guide provides easy access to OFB member farm stands.
